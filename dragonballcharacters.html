Dragon Ball Characters

Dragon Ball’s story is driven by powerful fighters, brilliant allies, and unforgettable villains. This page highlights these major characters. It also provides short descriptions for new fans to have a small understanding of Dragon Balls best characters. You will also find a what series each character is in. This page is great for new fans to get a understanding of the characters they will see in Dragon Ball.

Featured Characters

Goku

Goku: A cheerful martial artist who lives for training and challenges. He is the main character of Dragon Ball who always steps up to protect others when the world is threatened.

Series: Dragon Ball, Dragon Ball Z, Dragon Ball Gt, Dragon Ball Super, and Dragon Ball Daima

Vegeta

Vegeta: A proud rival of Goku whoses intense drive of becoming stronger pushes him to evolve beyond his past, becoming a key defender through discipline and determination.

Series: Dragon Ball, Dragon Ball Z, Dragon Ball Gt, Dragon Ball Super, and Dragon Ball Daima

Gohan

Gohan: A kind-hearted fighter and the son of Goku with massive potential who becomes most dangerous when protecting the people he cares about.

Series: Dragon Ball Z, Dragon Ball Gt, and Dragon Ball Super

Piccolo

Piccolo: A calm and strategic warrior known for mentoring others (such as Gohan) with a serious approach to training and battle decisions.

Series: Dragon Ball, Dragon Ball Z, Dragon Ball Gt, Dragon Ball Super, and Dragon Ball Daima

Bulma

Bulma: A genius inventor whose technology and planning support the team. She is the first person that Goku meets in Dragon Ball

Series: Dragon Ball, Dragon Ball Z, Dragon Ball Gt, Dragon Ball Super, and Dragon Ball Daima

Krillin

Krillin: A loyal friend of Goku and a skilled martial artist who consistently shows courage, even when facing opponents far stronger than him.

Series: Dragon Ball, Dragon Ball Z, Dragon Ball Gt, Dragon Ball Super, and Dragon Ball Daima

Future Trunks

Future Trunks: A determined fighter from the future who comes to warn the z fighters about the andriods. He is the son of Bluma and Vegeta

Series: Dragon Ball Z and Dragon Ball Super

Frieza

Frieza: A ruthless villain with a cold personality and enormous influence over some of the franchise’s most famous conflicts. He is the villian who caused the erasure of the sayians and their planetes

Series: Dragon Ball Z, Dragon Ball GT, and Dragon Ball Super

Cell

Cell: A terrifying andriod built around perfection and evolution. Cell vs Goku and Gohan is considered some of the best fights ever in anime.

Series: Dragon Ball Z

Majin Buu

Majin Buu: A powerful and unpredictable character whose presence shifts the story between chaos, comedy, and serious danger.

Series: Dragon Ball Z, Dragon Ball Gt, Dragon Ball Super, and Dragon Ball Daima

Beerus

Beerus: A God of Destruction who job is to balance the universe of mortals. He is known to be quite lazy but is one of the strongest people Goku has ever face.

Series: Dragon Ball Super

Whis

Whis: A Angel who is Beerus assiant and also mentor. He helps train Goku and Vegeta to reach powers they have never seen before.

Series: Dragon Ball Super

How to remember the cast

  • Heroes usually focus on training and protecting others. Example: Goku
  • Rivals push the heroes to improve and become stronger and often change over time. Example: Vegeta
  • Villains raise the stakes and create the biggest turning points. Example: Frizea
